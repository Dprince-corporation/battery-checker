about the creation of this website

The idea that lead to the creation of this website, i was just sitting at the desk, facing my laptop screen, thinking about how phone, laptop battery is draining so fast after 1 year of usage, so am like is there not a way to monitor battery degradation?, then i asked Google, i found answer related to (windows battery-report using: [powercfg /battery-report]). So i started thinking about the logic behind it. after days of thinking googling stuff, i got the idea if total capacity is given the the rest can be calculated using algorithm, that's how i decided to try it in JAVASCRIPT.
